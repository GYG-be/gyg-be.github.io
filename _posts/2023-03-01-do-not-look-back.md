You can improve the culture by being present, but you can also make things worse by choosing to live in the past. 

It comes down to a very simple choice really.

# Be present, elevate your discipline from where you are

# For today or right now, focus on NOW and elevating your current state

# Tomorrow you will need to be ready to focus on tomorrow's tasks of elevation

# Time goes one way, so focus your mind on planning on tomorrow rather than regretting yesterday

# The tough part is leaving people living in the past behind

Let the dead bury the dead. Although there are lessons from History, we are not going to get anywhere by reliving some highlight reel or some trauma that happened two or three or ten or twenty-five or fifty years ago. **Let the dead bury the dead.**
