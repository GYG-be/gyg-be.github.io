Every little thing that you hate doing is a lesson that helps you elevate your discipline.

# You are going to pay one way or another; you don't need to keep repaying.

# Enjoy the journey; embrace and study the suck

# Understand the lesson in order to expeditiously work through it

# Eliminate the root cause of the failure that sucked you into the suck

# The tough part is leaving people living in the root causes that drive the suck behind

Let the dead bury the dead. Although there are lessons from History, we are not going to get anywhere by reliving some highlight reel or some trauma that happened two or three or ten or twenty-five or fifty years ago. **Let the dead bury the dead.**
