## GYG is about continuously developing, continuously integrating, continuously deploying a BETTER you.

This framework is customized for YOUR purposes but it is a standard framework so that others can start with a bit of lead ... but you don't need to follow their vision or implement their vision ... GYG is only a values-agnostic generic framework ... it's up to YOU to develop your plan for you to envision, plan, build, maintain and pass on to others.

## The zeroth step is figure out what the DVCS of Git and Git flows are 

Git has become THE recognized standard for continuous development, continuous integration, continuous deployment ... there are other approaches, but they will all have features that are common to DVCS ... you can adapt Git or use a similar standard that works better for you -- but it's a waste of time to avoid using the standard.

You can BE GYG and transform GYG ... as you use it, you will develop better adaptations with revinventing the STANDARD wheel that works so well for other knowledgeworkers who rely upon continuous development, continuous integration, continous deployment ... part of breaking the bad habits of being you is about thinking about which of those fundmental habits really help you ... obviously, if those fundamental habits are REALLY helping you, you should use them ... otherwise, you will want use the continuous development, continuous integration, continuous deployment wheel that has been well-proven to effectively work for others whose work involves thinking, solving problems with others and having their thinking and development ideas carefully scrutinized in code reviews before it is tested in production.

## Git Your GYG ... the whole framework is recursively forkable and refactoring the framework is up to YOU.*

GYG is fundamentally about removing the bad habits of being yourself and replacing those habits with better habits of being a better you.

YOU are the only one who can do that ... and, if you are one of those who lacks humility and somehow imagines that you do not have any bad habits ... well, you *might just be the one who needs to work on yourself the most* ... in fact, there's not really any ***might*** about that, but we'll let you figure that out.  You are welcome to die believing your habits are perfect and, *we can be rather sure that people will be very glad to know your gone.*

Using the GYG framework is a very simple process ... you fork and clone the GYG.be framework ... then you can start journaling and listifying but you might have things that better work for you ... as you understand how the framework works, you can begin to revise, refactor and completely re-create the parts of the design that works better for you ... and your GYG becomes a template for others. 

## From the START be thinking about a BETTER way to continuously develop, continuously integrate, continuously deploy a BETTER you.

**GYG is designed for TESTING IN PRODUCTION.**  We don't waste time with trying to get everything perfect before we start testing ... GYG is designed to be a framework that removes the tedious parts of developing our vision, objectives, SMART goals and action plans ... but, as we go through standard processes, checklists and the different components of developing our action plans, we will have inherently thought through a lot of things *that others never think about* ... but when we have a plan, the point is to TEST IT RIGHT AWAY IN PRODUCTION, ie we don't wait until New Years Day to go to the gym or start on a diet.  

## TEST IN PRODUCTION ... design in the LISTENING and OBSERVABILITY to gather observations.

The GYG way is to test in production IMMEDIATELY *as soon as we have a plan* ... because GYG speeds up the the planning and deployment process. 

We want to TEST as soon as we can ... because, as any fighter well knows, *the plan for the fight is going to change when we get punched in the face.* The POINT is to get punched in the face AS SOON AS POSSIBLE ... *rather than going through life as a coward who will refuse to ever implement new plans because the coward might get punched in the face.*

# GYG is about MAINLY about implementing the habits of becoming a better you.

***GYG.be is INTENDED to be opportunity for you to EXTEND, BUILD, OWN and PERPETUATE your own transformative GYG.*** 

The GYG framework is designed to speed up the KNOWLEDGE processes of continuously developing, continuously integrating, continuously deploying an even BETTER you ... We want to TEST as soon as we can ... but we also want to AIM HIGHER with our vision and strategic goal setting, to ultimately deploy a more meaningful, more transformative, more impactful version of ourselves.

There might be plenty of room to start from scratch and develop something that is generally better and even more transformative than GYG.be ... maybe you have a better approach and we would use your approach if we knew about it.

## Based upon THE STANDARD in highly-collaborative developmental knowledge work

The de factor standards in Git-based CD/CI/CD Gitflow frameworks drive the foundation of GYG.be.

GYG.be is not really *our* framework, per se; rather, it a work-in-progress is based on building upon and extending a very much proven **continuously developed, continuously integrated, continuously deployed** set of best practices for the most innovative, most highly-collaborative knowledge-work which, in turn based on what has very much THE STANDARD in distributed development, the Git-based CD/CI/CD Gitflow frameworks.

If you have better ideas, we desperately want to hear bout those ideas ... but we have to GIT GOING with an proven standard that is recognized as standard way to THINK THROUGH continuously developing, continuously integrating, continuously deploying a better vision of ourselves.

GYG.be is fundamentally about becoming ***KNOWN*** as an open sourcist, about open sourcing your dev process and your tech, sharing your knowledge and your flaws ... GYG.be is about PUBLICLY making your ***virtual*** BETTER YOU available to HELP others ... because open sourcism is necessarily about the social activity helping others who, in all likelihood, will actually never thank you or even know who to thank.

The ***building a better you*** nature of GYGing is completely autodidactic ... it's about teaching in a virtual classroom of thousands or millions, because the person who learns the most in any classroom is the one who present the lesson. 

In the transformation of you, it's completely about **doing the problems** *in public* ... it's not for cowards ashamed of their habits and very much intent on hiding in the crowd ... GYG.be is really ONLY for self-starters who have some intention of becoming more courageous, but might just *Git by with some help from their open source friends.*
