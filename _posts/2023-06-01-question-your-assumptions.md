You should try to retire earlier than you think you can retire ... so that you have the time, energy and freedom to really question your unquestioned assumption.  

# There's way, way, way more to the Universe than humans can comprehend.

It's not JUST that the Universe is AT LEAST 95% OR MORE comprised of dark matter or things that humans don't understand. It's not just that the Universe is infinite and expanding. It's not just that humans tend to be EXTREMELY human centric and unaware of Nature or natural ecoystems.It's not just that most people barely really know anything about the 100 or so people they are comfortable around ... when there are over 8 billion different humans on the planet who are fully engaged in totally different creative, scientific, athletic and economic pursuits ...

# Get the fuck off your treadmill, before your treadmill running defines your life as a treadmill runner.
