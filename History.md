History ... is partly about Culture ... but it can also inform our Travels to certain areas of the world or our VIRTUAL travels into interactions with different people from a variety of different cultural institutions, such as seminaries or other places where history is still enough to get one killed or ostracized or dismissed.

Most of maybe about relaxing, just listening to audiobook or maybe looking at some Wikipedia pages while I take a break from what I was *working* on ... but sometimes it's a lot more serious and not really a matter of relaxing. Because history has a way of still being taken danged seriously by a lot of people ... and that means wars over stuff that happened 1000 years ago [as in Ukraine and Rus] or 2000 years ago [as in Palestine, the scattering of the Hebrews by a Roman conqueror and then Zionist return of the scattered, oppressed Jews to their homeland].

# History and Culture Listings

Our aim with developing listings is to never have to think about our next list of items to explore/annotate

I want to have roughly 250 to 125 good art exploration ideas in the hopper ... in order to to have 50 to 25 that we visit on at least once a month ... in order to have ten to five that we are currently touching on once a week ... and to have ONE main GOTO one that we are spending too much time with right now.

1) Hebrews, Jews, Zionism ... as well as the history of the Arab peoples as well as other Abrahamic religions, Christianity and Islam

2) [Rus, Ukrainine, slavic peoples](https://g.co/gemini/share/623f135df7cf)

3) China, Japan, Genghis Khan as well as Daoism, Confucianism, Buddhism, Shintoism, and the history of the Silk Road

4) India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka and the sub-continent along with Nepal, Bhutan, Tibet, the Himalayas and the development of Buddhism and Hinduism

5) Africa and African history prior to and the development of the slave trade and African diaspora in the Americas, the Caribbean, and Europe ... and that diaspora's influence on the development of the Americas and the Caribbean, particular in Jazz, the Blues, Rap and other musical idioms

6) Indigenous peoples of the Americas, the Caribbean, and the Pacific Islands, Australia, and New Zealand 

7) Meta Wikipedia, wikipedian editing culture, general wikiculture and its origins, even extending to Weiner's cybernetics the history of the internet as a missile targeting system and larger general rise of the machines

8) History of economics and political economics, especially the history of the development of capitalism and the FAILED philosophical history of the development of socialism and communism

9) History of science and technology

10) History of culture, especially popular culture, mass media and social media
